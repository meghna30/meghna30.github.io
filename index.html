Meghna Gummadi

Artificial Intelligence Researcher

I am a Doctoral Candidate in the CIS department at the University of Pennsylvania and a memeber of the Lifelong Machine Learning Group in the GRASP Lab advised by Prof. Eric Eaton. My research interests are in creating artificial intelligence agents that can continually learn throughout their lifetime. My recent focus has been on developing learning representations that enable such AI agents to detect novelty or uncertainty and accommodate it in a seamless fashion, an important requirement for reliable deployment in the real world. Before pursuing a PhD, I earned an MS in robotics from the University of Pennsylvania and a BS in Electrical Engineering from PES Institute of Technology.

You can reach me via my email, meghnag@seas.upenn.edu, and you can also find me on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Google Scholar.
